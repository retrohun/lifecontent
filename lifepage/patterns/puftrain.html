Puffer train
This was created simply by perturbing
the sides of a B-heptomino with two
LWSS's.  A B-heptomino is a naturally
occurring object that lurches forward at
the speed c/2 before its own debris
usually destroys it.  Not here!  The
LWSS keep it alive.  See BHEPTO, RAKE,
RAKE2.  For a super dirty puffer, see
LINEPUF.
