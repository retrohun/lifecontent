Wick stretcher
A c/4 spaceship pulls a wick (line of ants) which is attached
to a stabilizing end. Both ends were found by computer search
in 1992, the moving end by Hartmut Holzwart, and the
stabilizing end by Dean Hickerson.
See also EFENCE, TIRETRAX, AQUA25.
